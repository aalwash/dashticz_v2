Dashticz has evolved to the next version v2.0, with support for:

* Most Domoticz-devices
* Custom buttons (weather radar, radio)
* Temperature and Energy/Gas Usage (Toon Eneco, via Domoticz)
* Ziggo Mediabox (enter channel up/down or pause)
* Latest news
* Train and traffic info (dutch)
* Streaming Radio
* Multiple screens (swipe for next screen)
* Moon phases
* And many more.....
